Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026 · Effective immediately
Plain summary: We collect only what we need to provide the service. We do not sell your data. Uploaded videos are processed and permanently deleted within 24 hours. You have full control over your data. Regional rights (EU GDPR, Turkey KVKK, California CCPA, Brazil LGPD) are described in detail below.

1. Who We Are

Data Controller: Lumnex LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of Wyoming, USA.

We operate lumnex.ai, an AI-powered video dubbing, subtitle generation, and voice synthesis platform. Privacy questions: privacy@lumnex.ai.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To process your videos, audio, and text and deliver output files
  • To manage your account and subscription billing
  • To detect the appropriate language for your interface
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, alerts) — never marketing without explicit consent
  • To improve our service through aggregated, anonymized analytics

4. File Handling & Retention

Files you upload are stored temporarily solely to process your request. Output files are retained for up to 24 hours after processing completes, then permanently deleted from our servers. We do not use your content for advertising, do not sell it, and do not use it to train AI models.

During processing, files may pass through these third-party APIs:

  • Deepgram — speech-to-text (privacy policy)
  • DeepL — text translation (privacy policy)
  • ElevenLabs — voice synthesis & cloning (privacy policy)
  • Google Gemini — AI text editing (privacy policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (privacy policy)
  • Supabase — authentication & database (privacy policy)
  • Resend — transactional email (privacy policy)
  • ipapi.co — IP-based language detection, no data retained

5. Data Retention

  • Uploaded/output files: Deleted within 24 hours of processing
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active; deleted within 30 days of closure
  • Usage logs: 30-day rolling window
  • Payment records: As required by applicable law (typically 7 years)
  • Support communications: 2 years from last interaction

6. Cookies & Local Storage

  • Session cookie (Supabase): Keeps you logged in. Expires on logout or after 7 days of inactivity.
  • Language preference (localStorage): Stored locally in your browser — never sent to our servers.
  • Stripe cookies: Used by Stripe for payment security and fraud prevention.

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels, or third-party analytics platforms.

7. Security

We use HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) encryption, secure token storage via Supabase, rate limiting, and input validation. Our infrastructure runs on Railway (US region). In the event of a personal data breach affecting your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

8. Children

Our service is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/EEA/UK/Turkey). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Contact privacy@lumnex.ai if you believe a minor's data was submitted.

9. Your Rights by Region

🇪🇺 European Union, EEA & United Kingdom (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Legal bases for processing:

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing necessary to deliver the service
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): Security, fraud prevention, service improvement
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Marketing emails — withdrawable at any time
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Payment record retention required by law

Your rights: Access · Rectification · Erasure ("right to be forgotten") · Data portability · Objection · Restriction of processing · Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior processing)

International transfers: Your data may be transferred to the United States. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) adopted by the European Commission as the transfer mechanism. Our key processors are either EU-US Data Privacy Framework certified or covered by SCCs.

Breach notification: We will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours and affected users without undue delay in the event of a breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms.

Supervisory authority complaints: EU residents: edpb.europa.eu. UK residents: ico.org.uk.

To exercise any right: privacy@lumnex.ai — we respond within 30 days.

🇹🇷 Türkiye (KVKK — Kişisel Verilerin Korunması Kanunu)

Veri Sorumlusu: Lumnex LLC, Wyoming, ABD.

İşleme amaçları: Hizmet sunumu, abonelik yönetimi, güvenlik ve sahteciliğin önlenmesi, yasal yükümlülüklerin yerine getirilmesi.

Yurt dışı aktarım: Kişisel verileriniz, hizmet sunumu için ABD'deki altyapımıza ve işlemcilerimize aktarılmaktadır. Bu aktarımlar, KVKK'nın 9. maddesi kapsamında gerekli güvenlik tedbirleri alınarak gerçekleştirilmektedir.

KVKK Madde 11 kapsamındaki haklarınız:

  • Kişisel verilerinizin işlenip işlenmediğini öğrenme
  • İşlenmişse bilgi talep etme ve aktarıldığı üçüncü kişileri bilme
  • Eksik veya yanlış verilerin düzeltilmesini ve bildirilmesini isteme
  • Yasal şartlar çerçevesinde silinmesini veya yok edilmesini isteme
  • Otomatik sistemlerle aleyhinize sonuç doğuran işlemlere itiraz etme
  • Kanuna aykırı işleme nedeniyle uğranan zararın giderilmesini talep etme

Başvuru: privacy@lumnex.ai (Türkçe kabul edilmektedir). Kişisel Verileri Koruma Kurulu'na (kvkk.gov.tr) şikâyette bulunma hakkınız saklıdır.

🇺🇸 California (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of personal information collected, used, or disclosed in the past 12 months
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell or share personal information for behavioral advertising
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not deny service or charge different prices for exercising CCPA rights
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: We only use sensitive data (voice samples) to provide the requested service

To submit a request: privacy@lumnex.ai with subject "CCPA Request". We respond within 45 days.

Contact: privacy@lumnex.ai. You may also contact Brazil's ANPD at gov.br/anpd.
