Last updated: April 7, 2026 · Effective immediately
Summary: We collect only what we need to provide the service. We do not sell your data. Uploaded videos are processed and deleted within 24 hours. You have full control over your data.

1. Who We Are

[SITE_NAME] ("we", "our", "us") provides an AI-powered video dubbing and subtitle platform accessible at [SITE_URL]. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Account data: Email address and username when you register
  • Usage data: Minutes used, features accessed, browser type, device type
  • Uploaded files: Videos and audio files you upload for processing
  • Payment data: Handled entirely by Stripe — we never store card numbers
  • IP address: Used for language auto-detection and abuse prevention
  • Cookies: Session management and language preferences only

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To process your videos and deliver output files
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To detect the appropriate language for your interface
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, alerts) — not marketing without consent

4. Video & Audio File Handling

Files you upload are stored temporarily on our servers solely to process your request. Output files are stored for up to 24 hours after processing, then permanently deleted. We do not analyze, share, or use your content for training AI models.

Processed files may pass through third-party APIs (Deepgram for transcription, DeepL for translation, ElevenLabs for voice synthesis). Each provider has its own privacy policy and deletes your data per their retention policy.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Deepgram — speech-to-text transcription (privacy policy)
  • DeepL — text translation (privacy policy)
  • ElevenLabs — voice synthesis and cloning (privacy policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (privacy policy)
  • Supabase — user authentication and database (privacy policy)
  • ipapi.co — IP-based language detection, no data stored

6. Data Retention

  • Uploaded/output files: deleted within 24 hours
  • Account data: retained while your account is active
  • Usage logs: 30 days rolling
  • Payment records: as required by law (typically 7 years)

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are in the European Union, UK, or California, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restriction — limit how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@[SITE_DOMAIN]

8. Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Session cookie: Keeps you logged in (expires when browser closes)

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

9. Security
