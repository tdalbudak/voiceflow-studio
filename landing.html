What you can do
Everything in one studio.
From transcript to dubbed video — no switching between tools.
🎙️

AI Dubbing

Upload a video, pick a target language and voice — get a fully dubbed video with synced audio in minutes. 60+ languages supported.

🧬

Voice Cloning

Record 30 seconds of your voice or upload audio. The AI clones it exactly — dub videos with your own voice in any language.

💬

Auto-Subtitles

Deepgram AI transcribes your video with speaker detection. Style them freely, then burn into the video.

TikTok / Hormozi Mode

Word-by-word highlighting in bold TikTok style. The viral caption format used by top creators. One click to apply.

Transcript Editor

Click any segment to jump to that moment. Edit text, merge or split segments. Export as SRT, VTT, TXT or ASS.

Text-to-Speech

Type or paste text — choose from 30+ professional AI voices with emotion controls — download studio-quality audio instantly.
